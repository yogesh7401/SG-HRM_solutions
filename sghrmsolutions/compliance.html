Shops & Establishments Compliance

Most of the Industries covered with Shops & Establishment, it is imperative that employer follow all rules and regulations to be followed under Shops & Establishments Act. Being state specific act it is important for the employer to keep tract of region wise regulations and updates to be complaints and run a hassle free Business Operations.

Our Scope of Services under Shops & Establishment

  • Registration of Establishments under Shops & Establishments
  • Maintenance of Records and Registers
  • Guiding and advising the clients on adhering Minimum wages
  • Periodical & Timely submission of returns with respective Labour authorities
  • Liasioning with Labour department authorities
  • Court conciliation and attending cases
  • Advisory support on Minimum Wages, Vendor Audit and in required Regulations
  • Periodical communication about amendment in Labour Laws.
